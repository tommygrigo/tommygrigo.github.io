I am a young researcher from Italy.
I obtained my Ph.D. and Master degree at the Department of Information Engineering at the University of Padua where I am currently a postdoc.

My research pivots around theoretical and numerical methods to study and control complex dynamical systems, with particular focus to probability dynamical models, both classical and quantum.
