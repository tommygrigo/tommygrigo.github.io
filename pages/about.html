I am an Italian researcher.
I obtained my Ph.D. and Master degree from Department of Information Engineering, University of Padua where I am currently a postdoc.

My research lies at the intersection of control theory, applied mathematics, and quantum science and technology. In particular, I develop theoretical and computational methods for modelling, analysing, controlling and reducing quantum dynamical systems. More broadly, my research aims to establish systematic control-theoretic frameworks for understanding and engineering the challenges posed by emerging quantum technologies.
